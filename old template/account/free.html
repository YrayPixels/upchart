Free Funding

Get the chance to be funded with a live account by taking our free challenge. This funding is to help traders who can't afford our paid funding.





Description

This Funding is only available to 20 traders monthly and these traders are selected randomly. The challenge is a three phase challenge with profit targets of 10%, 8% and 5% respectively which are to be met at specific times. The maximum days for the whole challenge is 120 days with the minimum being 30 trading days. Once you meet the target for each phase, you are quickly moved to the next phase.



The aim of this is to correctly verify a trader before assisting them with the free funded account.



important details to note




1

5% Daily Drawdown


your account balance/equity must not be short below 5% daily, this value changes daily, as it is calculated as 5% of you closing balance or equity depending on which is higher for the day.


example


if you trade a $10,000 account, your total realized and unrealized PnL must not be below 5% of the starting balance which is $500 for that day. If at the end of the day you closed at $10,200 your new daily drawdown would be 5% of $10,200.




2

10% Overall Drawdown


your account balance/equity must not be below 10% of your starting balance at anytime.


example


if you trade a $10,000 account, your total realized and unrealized PnL must not be below 10% of the starting balance at any time.




3

10% Profit Target phase 1 and 8% profit target phase 2


You need to hit a profit target of 10% and also 8% within a calendar period of 30days each and not violating other rules before you move to the next phase.





4

5% Profit Target Verification Phase 3


You need to hit a profit target of 5% within a calendar period of 60days and not violating other rules before you move to the next phase.





5

10 Minimum trading days


This is neccessary so as to verify your trades during those 10days.





6

Unrestricted Trading style


All trading styles are permitted. However, good risk management is advised.
